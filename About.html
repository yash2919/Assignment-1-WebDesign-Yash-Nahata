Some interesting Facts about Movies-

  • The First Movie: The world's first motion picture, "Roundhay Garden Scene," was created by Louis Le Prince in 1888. It's only about 2.11 seconds long and consists of people walking in a garden.

  • Expensive Movie: As of my knowledge cutoff date in September 2021, "Avengers: Endgame" held the record for the most expensive movie ever made, with a reported budget of over $350 million.

  • Shortest Feature Film: "Durations" is recognized as the shortest feature film ever made. It has a runtime of just 27 seconds.

  • The Wilhelm Scream: A distinctive scream sound effect known as the "Wilhelm Scream" has been used in over 400 films, including "Star Wars," "Indiana Jones," and "Lord of the Rings."

  • Most Oscar Wins: "Titanic" and "The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King" share the record for the most Oscar wins, with 11 Academy Awards each.
